Tell me about yourself.

30秒自我介绍 — 面试官的第一印象，就在这30秒
📚 自习（你自己把控） 🎤 面试评估 15min 📅 Day 1

📋 Model Answer · 30 秒电梯演讲

目标：25-30秒。5层信息层层递进：身份 → 经验 → 规模 → 成就 → 目标。

🗣 标准版（跟读用）

Hello, I'm Jason Huang, Product Director specializing in payment hardware. I have 10 years of industry experience, growing from a PM to a department head. I oversee 6 product lines and more than 50 products, with annual sales hitting 8 million units shipped to over 70 countries. Our traditional POS products have held the No.1 market share in China for six consecutive years. Right now, I'm actively seeking a Product Director position based in Shenzhen.

📐 结构拆解（5层递进）

1. 身份："I'm Jason Huang, Product Director specializing in payment hardware."

2. 经验："10 years of industry experience, growing from a PM to a department head."

3. 规模："6 product lines, 50+ products, 8 million units/year, 70+ countries."

4. 成就："#1 market share in China for six consecutive years."

5. 目标："Actively seeking a Product Director position in Shenzhen."

📝 词汇卡片 · Chunk + 画面 + 发音

Product Director specializing in
🔊 PRO-duct Di-REC-tor SPE-cial-iz-ing in
不是 "I do payment hardware"，不是 "I am responsible for"。总监级的自我定位。
growing from a PM to a department head
🔊 GROW-ing from a PM to a de-PART-ment head
一句话讲完10年成长轨迹。不说"I started as...then I became..."太啰嗦。
oversee
🔊 o-ver-SEE
总监动词。"I oversee 6 product lines" 不是 "I am responsible for" 也不是 "I manage"。
six consecutive years
🔊 six con-SE-cu-tive years
亮点词。面试官耳朵会竖起来。必须说对，必须说稳。
8 million units
🔊 eight MIL-lion U-nits
不是 8 millions！不是 8 million unions！两个坑都要跳过。
70 countries
🔊 SEV-en-ty CUN-tries
复数！country → countries。数字后面名词加 s。
actively seeking
🔊 AC-tive-ly SEEK-ing
不是 "I want to find a job"，不是 "I'm looking for"。积极的、主动的。

🎯 语法锚点 · 数量表达 + 主谓一致

30秒介绍每一句都有数字。数字语法错一个 = 面试官立刻注意到。

数量表达三大坑
❌ 错误✅ 正确规则
8 millions units 8 million units 具体数字 + million（不加s）
70 country 70 countries 数字 > 1 → 名词复数
six consecutive year six consecutive years 同上，六年 = 复数
I leading the team I lead / I oversee 主语 I + 动词原形（不是ing）
数字=信号。看到具体数字 → 名词加 s → million/thousand/hundred 不加 s。

🔊 发音预警

consecutive → con-SE-cu-tive（重音在 SE，不是 CON）
specializing → SPE-cial-iz-ing（5音节，慢！）
department → de-PART-ment（不是 de-PART-men-T）
currently → CUR-rent-ly（两个音节，不是三个）
actively → AC-tive-ly（重音在第一音节）

🧠 检索练习 · 自习完成后自测

  1. 合上一切，打开手机录音
  2. 完整说出 30 秒自我介绍（目标 25-30 秒）
  3. 听录音。检查：百万 million 没加 s？countries 复数？consecutive 发音准？
  4. 找到最卡的 1 处 → 只练那里 × 5 遍
  5. 再完整录一遍 → 满意了 → 往下翻，复制 Prompt 去找 AI 面试官
