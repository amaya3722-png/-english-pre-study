Walk me through your career.

职业故事 · 三幕叙事——从PM到产品总监的十年成长
📚 自习（你自己把控） 🎤 面试评估 15min 📅 Day 2

📋 Model Answer · 三幕叙事

好故事不是念简历。有起点、转折、高潮。展现成长轨迹：执行者 → 管理者 → 战略者。

🎬 Act 1 · PM 时代（2016–2021）—— 起点

I started my career at NEWPOS in 2016, right after graduating with a degree in Automation. I joined as a Product Manager, focused on mobile POS products.

In those first five years, I was responsible for product go-to-market strategy and market promotion. I built the standardized product documentation system — sales collateral, user manuals, security specifications — which became the foundation for our pre-sales and bidding operations. I also supported multiple bidding projects as a technical advisor, helping the team win key contracts.

Those five years gave me a deep understanding of the entire payment chain. It laid the groundwork for everything that came next.

🎬 Act 2 · 总监跃升（2021–至今）—— 高潮（最长的一段！）

In 2021, after delivering strong results in product iteration and market expansion, I was promoted to lead the entire product department. This was a significant shift — from managing products to managing a product organization.

I inherited 6 product lines at various stages of maturity. My first priority was building the team — I grew it from 6 people to 12, structured into four functional groups: product planning, product marketing, pre-sales, and project management. I also built standardized workflows for product lifecycle management and bidding processes, shifting the department from ad-hoc to process-driven.

On the product side, I led several key transformations. I expanded our smart terminal portfolio from 2 aging models to 6 competitive products across Qualcomm, Unisoc, and MTK. I took our sound box from concept to 300,000 units in six months — our first product to enter the Alipay ecosystem. And I drove an AI-powered digital transformation that boosted pre-sales efficiency by over 50%.

Today, with self-developed products plus ODM partnerships, we have over 50 products in market, shipping 8 million units annually to more than 70 countries.

🎬 Act 3 · 为什么在这里—— 收尾（有力！）

After five years as a department head, I have taken the product organization from good to great. We have a strong team, mature processes, and a proven track record. Now I am ready for a new challenge at a larger scale.

What I bring is a combination that is hard to find in one person: deep payment hardware expertise, proven global market experience, a track record of building and leading teams, and genuine passion for using AI to make product organizations more efficient. I am looking for a Product Director role where I can apply that full stack of capabilities.

📝 词汇卡片 · 叙事专用 Chunk

laid the groundwork
🔊 LAID the GROUND-work
Act 1 收尾句。不是 "it helped me later"——是 "为后面的一切打下了基础"。
was promoted to lead
🔊 was pro-MO-ted to LEAD
被动语态自然用法。不是我"主动要"升职——是我"被"提拔。职业叙事的正确姿态。
inherited 6 product lines
🔊 in-HE-ri-ted
接手时各产品线成熟度参差不齐。不是 "I managed"，是 "我继承了一个需要整顿的局面"。
from ad-hoc to process-driven
🔊 from ad-HOC to PRO-cess DRI-ven
你最大的管理成就浓缩成一句话。人治→流程治理。
a significant shift
🔊 a sig-NI-fi-cant SHIFT
从管产品到管组织的转折点。不是 "a big change"——那是小学生说的。
full stack of capabilities
🔊 FULL STACK of ca-pa-BI-li-ties
你独特的组合——不是一两个强项，是一整套能力。面试官听完要知道：这个人是全套的。

🎯 语法锚点 · 全程过去式 + Act 2 是高潮

职业叙事 = 90% 过去式。Act 1 和 Act 2 全部是过去的事。只有 Act 3 的"我现在找什么"用现在时。

时态分区
段落时态信号词
Act 1 (2016-2021)全程过去式started, joined, built, became, laid
Act 2 (2021-present)混合：故事=过去式，现状=现在时was promoted, inherited, grew, expanded / today we have...
Act 3 (why here)现在完成 + 现在时have taken, am ready, I bring, I am looking
❌ 上节课的错✅ 正确
we have only two models (当时)we had only two models
I grow the team from 6 to 12I grew the team from 6 to 12
说到具体年份/过去的事件 → 动词自动切过去式。没有例外。

Act 2 必须是最长的一段！

Act 1 = 铺垫（3-4句）→ Act 2 = 高潮（6-8句，最多细节）→ Act 3 = 收尾（2-3句，有力）。面试官最关心的是你当了总监之后做了什么。

🔊 发音预警

inherited → in-HE-ri-ted（不是 in-he-ri-TED）
ad-hoc → ad-HOC（h 发音，不是 a-dock）
genuine → GEN-u-ine（3 音节，不是 gen-U-INE）
significant → sig-NI-fi-cant（重音在第二个音节）
maturity → ma-TU-ri-ty（不是 MA-tu-ri-ty）
documentation → do-cu-men-TA-tion（5 音节，慢！）

  3. 听录音。检查：Act 1 动词全是过去式？Act 2 是最长的一段吗？Act 3 收尾有力吗？
