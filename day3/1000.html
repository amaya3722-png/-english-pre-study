What exactly did you do to turn it around?

智能终端产品线转型 · Action 环节——STAR 最核心的一段
📚 自学 15 min 🎤 口语课 25 min 📅 Day 3

📋 Model Answer · B2 级理想回答

📋 Action 段 · 4 个关键行动

Here's what I did. First, I developed a three-generation product roadmap — moving from the single Qualcomm 8909 platform to a three-platform strategy covering Qualcomm, Unisoc, and MTK. This was the strategic backbone of the entire turnaround.

Second, I tailored the platform selection to different target markets. For Japan and Europe, we used Qualcomm for its reliability. For emerging markets in Asia, Africa, and Latin America, we used Unisoc and MTK to hit aggressive cost targets. This differentiated approach was something our competitors weren't doing.

Third, I drove the EMV Level 1 and Level 2 certifications, plus local certifications in multiple countries — Japan, Brazil, Europe. This was critical because without these certifications, we couldn't even enter those markets.

Finally, I created a modular PRD template system. Before this, every customer customization required writing a new PRD from scratch. The modular templates cut our customization delivery time significantly.

🎯 语法锚点 · 并列动词时态一致

Action 段是长叙事，多个动词并列。最容易犯的错 = 第一个动词过去式，第二个就忘了。

"and" 前后的动词必须时态一致
❌ 常见错误✅ 正确表达
I developed a roadmap and tailor the platform… I developed a roadmap and tailored the platform…
I drove the certification and create templates… I drove the certification and created templates…
The templates cut delivery time and become the standard The templates cut delivery time and became the standard
遇到 "and" → 回头看前面动词的时态 → 保持一致。
