What was the impact? And what would you do differently?

智能终端转型 · Result 量化 + 面试必考题：Second Conditional
📚 自学 15 min 🎤 口语课 25 min 📅 Day 3

📋 Model Answer · B2 级理想回答

📋 "What was the impact?"

The results were clear. We expanded the product line from just 2 models to 6, covering all major chipset platforms — Qualcomm, Unisoc, and MTK. Our overseas market share grew significantly, and we successfully entered major global markets including Japan, Europe, and Brazil.

More importantly, the modular PRD system I introduced became the standard across the entire product department. It reduced our customization turnaround time so much that the team still uses it today.

⚡ 追问："What would you do differently?"（必须用 Second Conditional！）

If I were to do it again, I would accelerate the multi-platform transition. We spent almost a year on the 8909 platform before fully committing to the three-platform strategy. If we had moved faster, we could have captured even more market share in the early stages.

🎯 语法锚点 · Second Conditional（面试杀手级语法）

面试中被问 "What would you do differently?" 必须用 second conditional。这是今天最重要的一节语法。

公式：If + past simple, would + base verb
❌ 错误✅ 正确
If I will do it again, I will accelerate… If I were to do it again, I would accelerate…
If I do it again, I will move faster If I did it again, I would move faster
If we moved faster, we will capture more If we had moved faster, we could have captured more
过去假设 → If + had done, would have done
现在假设 → If + were/did, would do

🔥 面试场景速查：
"If I were to join your team…"
"If you gave me the opportunity…"
"If we had started earlier…"
