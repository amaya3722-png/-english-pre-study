📋 完整 STAR · 智能终端转型（1.5–2 分钟版）

📋 标准回答："Tell me about a time you turned around a struggling product line."

SITUATION When I took over our smart terminal line in 2021, it was struggling. We had only two models, both on the outdated Qualcomm 8909 platform. The designs looked old, and we were losing ground.

TASK I needed to transform it into a competitive, multi-platform portfolio.

ACTION I developed a three-generation product roadmap, moving from that single platform to a three-platform strategy across Qualcomm, Unisoc, and MTK. I then tailored the platform choice to each target market — Qualcomm for Japan and Europe, Unisoc and MTK for cost-sensitive emerging markets. I drove the EMV L1/L2 and multi-country local certifications. And I created a modular PRD template system that significantly reduced customization turnaround time.

RESULT The product line went from 2 models to 6. We entered major global markets including Japan, Europe, and Brazil. And the modular PRD system I built became the department standard — it's still in use today. If I were to do it again, I would accelerate the multi-platform transition even faster, because the first-mover advantage in some of those markets was huge.

🔄 同一项目 × 3 种面试问题

今天的核心训练：同一个 STORY，针对不同问题调整 Focus。不是背三套答案，是一套答案换三个角度讲。

题 1："Turn around a struggling product line."

🎯 焦点：战略决策 — 为什么选三平台而不是双平台？怎么想的？

S 强调"濒临淘汰"，A 强调"3 代路线图+差异化平台"，R 强调"从 2 到 6"

题 2："Manage complexity across multiple platforms."

🎯 焦点：管理能力 — 三个平台同时跑，怎么协调？

S 强调"同时管 6 款产品"的复杂度，A 强调"模块化 PRD + 多团队协调"，R 强调"成为部门标准"

题 3："Expand into new markets."

🎯 焦点：全球化 — 怎么进入日本/欧洲/巴西的？

S 强调"海外份额低"，A 强调"认证+本地化+差异化选型"，R 强调"进入日本/欧洲/巴西"
