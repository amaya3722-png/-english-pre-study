Step 1 · 自习 · Self-Study

Tell me about a time you built a product from scratch.

STAR 方法 · Sound Box 项目 —— 从零到30万台，S→T→A→R 完整叙事
📚 自习（你自己把控） 🎤 面试评估 15min 📅 Day 3

📋 Model Answer · 完整 STAR 回答

S=两句话 · T=一句话两步走 · A=6个动词全部过去式 · R=数字+策略意义。一镜到底。

🔴 S · Situation — 两句话，不啰嗦

Alipay and WeChat Pay have extremely high barriers for hardware vendors to enter their ecosystem. Our company had never been part of their supplier network before — we had zero presence in audio payment terminals, while competitors already had a first-mover advantage.

🎯 T · Task — 一句话，两步走

My task was a two-step process — first, get onto Alipay and WeChat Pay's short-term supplier lists just to get a foot in the door; second, get our product formally qualified and become a long-term supplier for both platforms.

🟡 A · Action — 6个关键动作（全部过去式！）

I did several things in parallel. First, I leveraged senior-level relationships to get us into Alipay's first batch of pilot partners — without that, we would not even have had a seat at the table.

Then I drafted the full PRD and technical specifications based on customer requirements. I coordinated with the industrial design team on the hardware appearance. I personally drove the IC selection and BOM cost optimization to hit our target price point.

The most challenging part was coordination. I set up a cross-functional sync mechanism across four teams — product, R&D, sales, and Alipay — to keep everyone aligned. At the same time, I pushed forward the WeChat Pay product qualification in parallel, so we would not be locked into a single ecosystem.

🟢 R · Result — 数字 + 策略意义

The product launched and shipped over 300,000 units within six months. It became our company's first product to enter the Alipay ecosystem. We simultaneously secured formal WeChat Pay qualification — avoiding dependency on a single ecosystem. This project gave our company a much stronger and more established market position in the sound box product category.

🎯 语法锚点 · STAR 全程过去式

⚠️ Day 1 + Day 2 两节课最弱项都是 Grammar(70%)——时态切换和冠词缺失。今天 STAR 几乎全程过去式，是纠正好时机。

S/T/A/R 的时态规则
环节时态为什么
S · Situation过去式"当时的局面"——已经发生了
T · Task过去式"我当时的目标"——已经完成了
A · Action🔴 全部过去式6个动词：leveraged / drafted / coordinated / drove / set up / pushed
R · Result过去式"当时的结果"——300,000 units shipped
❌ 连续两天犯的错✅ 正确
we have only two modelswe had only two models
漏掉冠词：became first productbecame the first product
I draft the PRD and coordinate with designI drafted the PRD and coordinated with design
STAR = 昨天发生的事 = 所有动词过去式。说完每个动词 → 脑子里闪过 -ed。没有例外。

🔊 发音预警

leveraged → LEV-er-aged（不是 le-VER-aged）
drafted → DRAF-ted（一个音节+d，不是 DRAF-ted）
cross-functional → cross-FUNC-tion-al（5音节，慢！）
simultaneously → si-mul-TA-ne-ous-ly
ecosystem → EE-co-sys-tem（不是 e-co-SYS-tem）
qualification → qua-li-fi-CA-tion
