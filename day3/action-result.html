Tell me what you DID and what was the RESULT

云音箱项目 · Action（你做的具体动作）+ Result（量化结果）—— STAR 最核心的两段
📚 自学 15 min 🎤 口语课 25 min 📅 Day 3

📋 Model Answer · B2 级完整 STAR

🗣 Situation（你已会 ✅ · 两句话）

In 2023, our company wanted to enter Alipay's audio payment ecosystem. The certification barrier was extremely high, and a competitor already had first-mover advantage.

🎯 Task（你已会 ✅ · 一句话带数字）

My goal was to get our Cloud Speaker certified and launched as one of Alipay's first batch partners — targeting 300,000 units within the first six months.

🔴 Action（今天重点 · 6 个关键动作）

First, I leveraged our executive-level relationships to get us into Alipay's pilot program. Once we were in, I wrote the full PRD and technical specifications, then coordinated with the ID design team to finalize the enclosure. I personally drove the IC selection and BOM cost control to keep the unit economics viable. To keep everything moving in parallel, I set up a cross-functional sync across four teams — product, R&D, sales, and Ant Group. At the same time, I pushed for WeChat Pay product qualification so we wouldn't be locked into a single ecosystem.

🔴 Result（今天重点 · 数字 + 策略意义）

The product shipped over 300,000 units in the first six months. It became our company's first-ever product inside the Alipay ecosystem, and we simultaneously secured WeChat Pay qualification. More importantly, we established our market position in audio payment terminals from scratch — going from zero to a recognized player in a category that didn't exist for us before.

🎯 语法锚点 · Action 段过去式一致性

今天只盯一件事：Action 段的每一个动词必须是过去式。

描述过去项目的每一句，动词检查有没有 -ed 或不规则变形
❌ 我常犯的✅ 正确表达
I leverage our relationships and get us into the program I leveraged our relationships and got us into the program
I drive the IC selection and coordinate with design I drove the IC selection and coordinated with design
I set up a cross-functional sync and push for WeChat Pay I set up a cross-functional sync and pushed for WeChat Pay
每说完一个动词 → 脑子里闪过 -ed。说 Action 段的时候，这就是你的唯一任务。

📝 Day 1 · 自我介绍（30s 核心骨架）

Hello, I'm Jason Huang, Product Director specializing in payment hardware. I have 10 years of industry experience, growing from a PM to a department head. I oversee 6 product lines and more than 50 products, with annual sales hitting 8 million units shipped to over 70 countries. Our traditional POS products have held the No.1 market share in China for six consecutive years. Right now, I'm actively seeking a Product Director position based in Shenzhen.

🎯 重点词：consecutive ★★★★☆ · oversee（替代 responsible for）· 8 million units（不是 millions）· 70 countries（复数！）

📝 Day 2 · 职业故事（三幕叙事）

Act 1 (2016-2021): I joined as a Product Manager in 2016. I was responsible for product promotion, bidding support, and building our product documentation system. Those years gave me deep knowledge of the full payment chain.

Act 2 (2021-present): I got promoted to department head in 2021. I expanded our product lines from 2 to 6, built a 12-person team across 4 functions, and led our AI digitization initiative that boosted efficiency by over 50%.

Act 3 (Why I'm here): I'm looking for a bigger platform where I can bring my end-to-end experience — hardware + payment + overseas + team leadership + AI — to create more commercial value.

🎯 关键：Act 2 要比 Act 1 长！| 结尾声音要稳，不要变小 | 过渡词：started as → then in 2021 → currently

📝 Day 3 · STAR S+T（已掌握 ✅）

S: In 2023, our company wanted to enter Alipay's audio payment ecosystem. The certification barrier was extremely high, and a competitor already had first-mover advantage.

T: My goal was to get our Cloud Speaker certified and launched — targeting 300,000 units within six months.

🎯 S=两句话（不啰嗦）| T=一句话带数字 | 全程过去式
